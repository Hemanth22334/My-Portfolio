The Best Movies According to Hemanth

My Top 6 Movies of All Time

Dil Bechara — Rating: 4.9

"Dil Bechara" is an emotional romantic drama that tells the story of two young souls, Kizie and Manny, who find love and meaning in life while battling their own painful realities.

Adapted from *The Fault in Our Stars*, the film is a beautiful journey of joy, pain, friendship, and letting go — reminding us to live and love deeply, even if life is short.

It also marks the unforgettable final performance of Sushant Singh Rajput, making it all the more touching.

A tender love story that teaches us to live and love deeply, even in limited time.

Chhichhore — Rating: 4.7

"Chhichhore" is a heartwarming Bollywood film that beautifully blends college nostalgia, friendship, and life's tough realities.

It follows a group of former hostel friends who reunite years later to support a friend's son during a crisis, sharing their unforgettable college journey filled with fun, failure, and resilience.

The movie delivers a powerful message — success is not everything, and failure is never the end.

A celebration of friendship, failure, and the strength to rise again.

3 Idiots — Rating: 4.5

"3 Idiots" is an inspiring and heartwarming film that follows the journey of three engineering students as they navigate friendship, pressure, and self-discovery.

Through humor and emotion, the film challenges the rigid education system and reminds us that passion, not pressure, leads to true success.

"3 Idiots" teaches us that excellence follows when you chase what you love — not marks or ranks.

Kai Po Che! — Rating: 4.5

"Kai Po Che!" is a gripping drama based on Chetan Bhagat's novel *The 3 Mistakes of My Life*. Set in Gujarat, it follows the story of three close friends—Govind, Ishaan, and Omi—who dream of starting a sports shop and cricket academy.

As they chase their goals, their friendship is tested by political unrest, natural disasters, and personal tragedies.

A moving tale of friendship, dreams, and resilience tested by fate and fractured by reality.

MS Dhoni: The Untold Story — Rating: 4.8

"M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story" is an inspiring biographical film that chronicles the life of Mahendra Singh Dhoni — from a small-town boy with big dreams to becoming one of India's most legendary cricket captains.

The movie dives deep into his personal struggles, relentless determination, and untold sacrifices behind his rise to glory.

The rise of a small-town boy who became a legend through grit, patience, and belief.

Dil Bechara — Rating: 4.9

Emotional and good narration — a soulful journey like in “3 Idiots,” but more inward.

Heart-touching, simple, and sincere; emotions land and the narration flows. Feel-good vibes with grounded emotions and neatly woven storytelling.

Quietly powerful — clean narration, steady emotions, lasting impact.

Warm, emotional arc with smooth narration and zero fluff.
